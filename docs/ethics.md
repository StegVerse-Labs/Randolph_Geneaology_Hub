# Ethics & Privacy Charter

## Core commitments
1. **Living people are private by default.**
2. **Consent is required** to make any living personâ€™s record public.
3. **Evidence over rumor:** claims must be sourced or clearly labeled.

## Why this matters
Family history should preserve dignity, reduce harm, and remain truthful over time.
